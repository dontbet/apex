How to play

  • Each round starts at 1.00x and grows until it crashes at a random point.
  • Place your bet during countdown. When accepted, it joins the next round.
  • Press Cash Out before crash to lock your current payout.
  • Pod booster: buy it before the round, then press DROP POD during flight to save that pod value while your main bet keeps running.
  • Hypersonic booster: buy it before the round to enter faster, higher-risk rounds that can reach much bigger multipliers.
